Her Story

Nara is 17, the friend who narrates everything, with a sketchbook in every bag. She grew up between Filipino family gatherings and Italian summers, and carries both rhythms into her painting.

She loves botanical references, terrazzo textures, and handwritten notes taped over fresh paint. Each piece is a small diary page.

“Art is my way of keeping memories in bloom.”
